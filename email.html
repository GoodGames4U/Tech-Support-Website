How To Send An Email


To send an E-mail, we will begin by opening Google Chrome. Once you are there search for your E-Mail server. I use G-mail so I went to 'Gmail.com'.
Log in once you are on your E-mail's website. If you need help logging in please reference the page 'How To Login to Social Media'.

If you are using another E-mail service provider, the process isnÂ´t too much different. In gmail I looked for the pencil on the left hand side.


When you get close to the pencil the sidebar will open more. Click on 'Compose'.


It will open up a new window. On this new window fill in the recipient's E-mail, the message subject, and the message. After typing your message, click 'Send'.

To see if your message, on the left side of the page, under the pencil search 'sent'.
Click on 'sent' and your recntly sent message should appear on top.

Congratulations! You have sent an E-mail!
