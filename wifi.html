How To Connect To Wi-Fi


To connect to wi-fi, look for the setting in the bottom lower right hand corner, and click where indicated in the picture.


Next, click on the setting indicated in the picture. This will open up your wi-fi settings.


Look for your conection within the avaliable connections. When you find your connection, click on it and put in your wi-fi password. If the password is incorrect, you will be advised. If this happens, double check your credentials and try again. Passwords are case sensitive and it is easy to type it incorrectly.


Congratulations, you are connected to the internet!
